Fidonet Information

Fidonet is an international amateur network of computer systems, often running public access bbs's, and all connected by modem (& often via the internet as well).

Fidonet links

Fidonet Information

For information about Fidonet itself, you can start by going to http://www.fidonet.org.
For Fidonet News, you can go to http://www.fidonews.org.
For information about Fidonet Technical Standards, you can go to http://www.ftsc.org.
For information about the Fidonet nodelist over time, you can go to http://goodolddaysbbs.etowns.net/fidonet.htm. Or to http://fidonet-on-the-internet.org, which also has that information online.

Fidonet Echos

  • For an extensive listing of the echos carried on Fidonet or othernets, go to http://www.tlchost.net/echolist/..
